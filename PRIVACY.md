# WordClip Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

## What WordClip Does
WordClip is a Chrome extension that helps users learn English by finding YouTube videos for highlighted words.

## Data Collection
WordClip does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data.

## How It Works
- When you highlight a word, WordClip sends that word to our server to search YouTube
- No personal information is sent
- No browsing history is stored
- No user accounts required

## Third Party Services
WordClip uses:
- YouTube Data API to find relevant videos
- Our own server (wordclip.duckdns.org) to process searches
